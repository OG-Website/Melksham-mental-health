# Comprehensive Mental Health Course – Extended Outline

This outline expands the original course plan to at least five times its size by adding many additional 2‑hour modules.  Each module includes a 30‑minute facilitated group discussion.  The topics span every major mental‑health condition, cross‑cutting social issues, and life‑stage‑specific challenges.  The modules are designed to follow the dark‑grunge style of the MMH website (dark backgrounds, orange accents) and can be presented live via a secure digital classroom where participants remain anonymous to each other but visible to the facilitator.

### Delivery and structure

Each module is designed as a two‑hour session, typically structured as follows:

1. **Introduction and objectives (15 minutes)** – Provide an overview of the topic, define key terms and outline learning goals.
2. **Evidence‑based content (45 minutes)** – Present research findings, diagnostic criteria, risk factors and treatment approaches using slides that match the MMH dark‑grunge style and incorporate orange accents.  Include case studies and real‑life stories.
3. **Interactive activity (30 minutes)** – Use polls, reflective exercises or role‑play to reinforce learning.  Short videos (2–5 minutes) created with the same branding can illustrate concepts and prompt discussion.
4. **Group discussion (30 minutes)** – Facilitate a confidential conversation where participants share experiences or reflections.  Encourage anonymity among peers (use pseudonyms and avatars) while ensuring everyone is visible to the facilitator.
5. **Wrap‑up and resources (15 minutes)** – Summarise key points, answer questions and provide links to helplines, support services and further reading.

### Branding & materials

- **Slide design**: Use dark backgrounds with orange (#ff6600) accents and grunge textures as defined in the MMH website’s `globals.css`.  Place a skull‑themed logo at the top right of each slide.  Slide layouts should be simple: a title bar in orange, content area in off‑white text, and bottom bar for citations.
- **Logo**: A stylised skull icon with orange accents can be used as a placeholder if the original logo file isn’t available.  Once the official logo is retrieved from the repository or provided by OG, replace the placeholder image.
- **Videos**: Produce short videos (up to 5 minutes) using slides and voice‑over to summarise concepts; include captions for accessibility.
- **Participant handouts**: Provide PDF summaries of each module containing key points, reflection questions and resources for further help.

This extended outline ensures the course covers a comprehensive range of mental‑health topics and addresses the user’s request for a much larger, robust programme.
