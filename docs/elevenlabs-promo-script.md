# ElevenLabs Promotional Video Script — 20 Seconds

Use this script with [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io) to generate the voiceover for a 20-second promotional video for the Melksham Mental Health Members Portal and 50-module course programme.

---

## Script (~20 seconds / ~45 words)

> Melksham Mental Health's new Members Portal is live — with 50 evidence-based modules covering anxiety, depression, trauma, burnout, relationships, and beyond. Live facilitated sessions. Real support. Built from lived experience. Join for free and start your journey today at melksham-mentalhealth dot u-s slash portal slash register.

---

## ElevenLabs Settings (recommended)

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Voice | Warm, calm, empathetic — e.g. "Bella" or "Rachel" |
| Stability | 0.65 |
| Clarity / Similarity | 0.75 |
| Style | 0.30 |
| Speaking Rate | Slightly slower (0.9×) for impact |

---

## Video Production Notes

- **Duration target**: 18–22 seconds
- **Tone**: Warm, hopeful, empowering — not clinical
- **Visuals suggestion**: Text overlays of the 50 module count, fade in of the portal homepage, end card with the URL and a "Join Free" call-to-action
- **Background music**: Soft, uplifting instrumental (no lyrics)
- **URL to show on screen**: melksham-mentalhealth.us/portal/register

---

## Disclaimer

Ensure the final video includes a brief on-screen note:  
*"This programme is educational and peer-supported. It does not replace professional medical or therapeutic care."*
